# Onboarding Playbook

New team workspaces receive a guided onboarding session after the first three
seats are activated. The onboarding owner sends a checklist that covers support
channels, audit-log retention, and sandbox limits.

SCIM provisioning is enabled only after the customer's domain is verified and a
test user sync succeeds. The support operations queue owns failed SCIM sync
triage until identity provider metadata is corrected.

Bulk ticket imports run during a scheduled import window. The customer must
upload a sample CSV at least two business days before the window so support
operations can validate field mappings.

Dedicated sandbox regions require the enterprise testing addendum and approval
from the onboarding owner. Sandbox regions are not enabled by the billing queue.
