# Support Desk Policy

The support desk answers questions about Atlas Helpdesk accounts. Support agents
can cite billing notes, escalation runbooks, and retrieval quality notes, but
they must not invent policy that is missing from the knowledge base.

Customers on the starter plan can open email tickets. Customers on the team plan
can open email tickets and shared Slack tickets. Enterprise customers also get a
named incident channel during Severity 1 incidents.

When an answer depends on a recent incident, the agent should cite the
escalation runbook and mention the dashboard checked by the on-call engineer.

Support agents may summarize known policy, but they must not invent policy that
is missing from the knowledge base. If the answer is not covered, the agent
should say that the policy is not documented and route the question to the
support operations queue.

Starter customers can request one active sandbox. Team customers can request
three active sandboxes. Enterprise customers can request dedicated sandbox
regions when the contract includes the enterprise testing addendum.
